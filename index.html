Newton's Universe
Space is a fixed, neutral stage. Time flows at a constant rate everywhere. Objects move through it — pulled by forces acting at a distance. Gravity is a force between masses. The universe is a billiard table and the laws are geometry.
Einstein's Universe
Space and time are one fabric — spacetime. Mass tells that fabric how to curve. The curved fabric tells mass how to move. Gravity is not a force. It is the shape of space.

There is no force pulling you to Earth. You are following the straightest possible path through curved spacetime. That path happens to look like falling.

Left side: the geometry of spacetime — how curved it is.

They are equal. They are the same statement. The distribution of mass-energy is the geometry of spacetime. This single equation replaced Newton's entire framework.
E = mc²
Not just a formula. A statement that mass and energy are the same thing in different form. A small amount of mass contains an enormous amount of energy — multiplied by the speed of light squared.

The c² is not a coincidence. It is the conversion rate between two things that were always one thing.

GPS Proof
GPS satellites orbit at 20,200 km. Weaker gravity means their clocks run 38 microseconds faster per day than clocks on Earth's surface.

Without correction, GPS would drift by 11 kilometers per day. The correction runs constantly. Your phone depends on Einstein being right.
The Fact

Light Paths
Light does not bend. Light travels in straight lines. But straight lines through curved space appear bent to any flat observer.

This was confirmed in 1919 — starlight bending around the Sun during a solar eclipse. The first experimental proof that Einstein was right.
Frame Dragging
A spinning mass drags spacetime around it like a spoon through honey. Objects near a rotating mass are pulled not just toward it but around it.

Measured directly by Gravity Probe B in 2011 after 40 years of development. The frame drag of Earth's rotation is 0.039 arcseconds per year. Confirmed to within 19%.

Not a surface. Not a wall. A geometry. The shape of spacetime closes inward completely. Nothing escapes because there is no outward direction left.
